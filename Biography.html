Biography of BMTH

A little bit from everyone

Overall

Formed in Sheffield, 2004, Bring Me The Horizon was a deathcore band consisting of four members, lead singer Oli Sykes, drummer Matt Nicholls, guitarist Lee Malia, bassist Matt Kean.

Their debut EP, "This Is What the Edge of Your Seat Was Made For" didnt see much success, unlike their later albums. As their career went on they went through a lot of changes, the most noticeable being from their 2nd and 3rd album, feeling lighter and more produced. Throughout they years they went from deathcore/screamo to metalcore, to hard-rock, to pop, to metalcore again. Jordan Fish was the reason for improvement in production over the years.

BMTH was influenced by At The Gates, Carcass, Pantera, Metallica, Zao, Arch Enemy, The Dilinger Escape Plan, Everytime I Die, Norma Jean, Skycamefalling and Poison The Well. Later influences were Twenty One Pilots, Linkin Park's Hybrid Theory, Killswitch Engage's Alive or Just Breathing, Panic! At The Disco, Radiohead and many more.

Oli Sykes

In His early life he moved to Australia as an infant for about 6 years. During his highschool years he released short tracks under the name Quakebeat. He also played in the hip-hop band "Womb 2 Da Tomb" with his brother and another soon-to-be BMTH member. Later he played in "Purple Curto" under the name Olisaurus, that later became his solo project's name. In 2004 he formed Bring Me The Horizon.

He also features in many songs, like "Even If You Win, You're Still a Rat", "Admiral's Arms" by Architects, "Silence Speaks" with While She Sleeps, "Code Mistake" with CORPSE or "Bad Life" with Sigrid. He performed with Linkin Park in 2017. In 2014 he got addicted to ketamine losing a lot of his voice, and singing way more than screaming. He got his voice back by now, but its still scary how it could've been the end of the band. He is married to Alissa Salls, featuring in some songs of BMTH like Parasite Eve. Medicine is a song about their relationship.

Matt Nicholls

He was born in Rotherham, South Yorkshire. His parents divorced early and his dad bought him a drum kit that he thaught himself on how to play. He was never influenced by any drummer, and never had any plan to be a drummer. He played in "Womb 2 Da Tomb" too. He joined BMTH in 2004, and is featured on every album they had. In the 2010s he had a concert where he broke his arm and Dan Searle of Architects had to fill in for him. His style in the early days consisted of fast playing influenced by Swedish metal. His style changed with the years. In 2019 he received the accolade of being the 10th best drummer. He is diagnosed with ADD, and hes a football fan.

Lee Malia

Also born in the Sheffield, South Yorkshire, Lee Malia started learning guitar at a young age. His parents were musicians, and his dad used to play guitar too. He was influenced by Gary Moore, Bon Iver, Pantera and Metallica as he listened to a lot of classic rock and metal. Later on he credited Cannibal Corpse that he drew heavier influences from. He wanted to learn the riff of Enter Sandman, and Master Of Puppets first.

He met Oli in Stocksbridge High School. He first played in a cover band that's first cover was of Blink-182's "All The Small Things". He played in a Metallica cover band before joining Bring Me The Horizon. He has really creative riffs and a unique playing style. He made music for two different movies by Adam MacDonald.

He started off playing an Ibanez, later he switched to an Epiphone. In studio he plays a Les Paul Standard. He usually uses C standard or drop A# tuning that makes for a heavy sound. He married Deni Marie McGonigle. In 2018 he got an accolade for being the 6th best metal guitarist.

Matt Kean

Matt Kean was also born in Sheffield. Growing up he wanted to be a footballer, but he thought thats never gonna happen. He studied Biology, Physics and Chemistry A levels. He started playing bass in College as another past time, to not just play computer games. He played stuff like Green Day and Blink-182. He watched Lee Malia perform in the Metallica tribute band and later joined BMTH.

He was the assumed manager of the band along with Oli Sykes' mom. He appeared on the front cover of the Bass Guitar Magazine. He uses a Gibbson guitar, and doesnt use any effects. He is a self-thaught bassist. He usually follows Malia's guitar parts to write the bass line. He is also vegan and a Manchester City football fan. He is dating the american singer Tillie.
